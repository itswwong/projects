About Us

About us

Our Club

We are a group of passionate Carlmont students who wish to help our peers reach their academic goals. We teach a wide range of subjects from math to French.

Although COVID-19 has presented us with educational challenges, we aim to turn the obstacles of distance learning into a positive experience for all and make 2020-2021 a great school year!

Why Founded

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide a safe environment for students of all grades and classes to support one another through collaborative tutoring.

We believe the best way to learn something is by teaching it, and that peers asking each other for help during this difficult time will boost community spirit.
