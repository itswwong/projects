Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (homemade/store bought)
  • half a bag of shredded mozzarella
  • half a tomato, sliced
  • half a jar of tomato sauce
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 tablespoons olive-oil
  • dried oregano

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Oil baking tray to prevent dough from sticking.
  3. Stretch dough into a circle to fit the baking tray.
  4. Spread tomato sauce and top with fresh tomatos and ham.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella on top.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes in pre-heated oven.
  7. Take it out, slice, and enjoy!
