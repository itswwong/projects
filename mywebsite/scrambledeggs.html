Scrambled Eggs

Serves 3.

Ingredients

  • 7-9 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons organic butter
  • half an onion, sliced
  • 2 chicken-apple sausages, sliced
  • 1 tablespoons olive-oil
  • sea salt
  • freshly-ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Slice onion and sausage.
  2. Heat non-stick pan over medium-heat.
  3. Beat eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl until blended.
  4. Drizzle olive-oil onto hot pan.
  5. Throw in onion and sausage.
  6. Pour in egg mixture after 2-3 minutes or until onions are golden-brown.
  7. Wait 10 seconds and begin stirring in a clockwise direction (prevent eggs from sticking to pan and creates smooth texture).
  8. Stir for 20 seconds then rest for 20 seconds.
  9. Throw in butter.
  10. Take the eggs off the heat and stir for 20 seconds.
  11. Repeat step 6 until eggs aren't runny.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
