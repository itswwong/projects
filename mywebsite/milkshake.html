Milkshake

Based on The Perfect Milkshake By Makinze Gore (delish)

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 cups) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
