Over Easy Pan-fried Egg

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • sea salt
  • freshly-ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoons olive-oil

Directions

  1. Heat non-stick pan over medium-heat.
  2. Drizzle olive-oil on hot pan.
  3. Crack egg onto pan.
  4. Generously season top of the egg with salt and pepper.
  5. Flip egg after 2 minutes or until slightly brown.
  6. Cook on other side for 30 seconds.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
