Mashed Potatoes

Source: Basic Mashed Potatoes by Esmee Williams (allrecipes)

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons organic butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • sea salt
  • freshly-ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm (about 15 minutes); drain.
  3. Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creamy.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
