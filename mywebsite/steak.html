Steak

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 New-York/Rib-eye Beef Steak
  • 2 tablespoons organic butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (with skin)
  • 1 sprig of fresh rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons olive-oil
  • sea salt
  • freshly-ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat cast-iron skillet over medium-heat.
  2. Heavily season steak with salt and black pepper right before cooking.
  3. Drizzle 2 tablespoons of olive-oil on hot skillet.
  4. Lay steak onto skillet.
  5. Drizzle 1 more tablespoon of olive-oil onto top-side of steak.
  6. Flip steak after 2 minutes or until it has a nice sear.
  7. Toss in butter, garlic, and rosemary.
  8. Baste butter onto steak a few times.
  9. Cover the skillet with a lid and sear for 2 minutes.
  10. Remove the lid, take the skillet off the heat, and let the steak rest for at least ten minutes.
  11. Serve and enjoy!
