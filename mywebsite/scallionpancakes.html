Scallion Pancakes

Description

Ingredients

  • 250 grams flour
  • 120 ml boiling water
  • 50-60 ml cold water
  • 50 grams olive-oil
  • 1 tablespoons olive-oil
  • 2 whole shallots, chopped
  • sea-salt

    Directions

    1. Put flour into a bowl, add boiling water, stir evenly.
    2. Add cold water, knead flour until the surface is smooth, cover the bowl with plastic wrap, let it rest for 30 minutes.
    3. Knead the dough into strips, divide it into 25 gram pieces and roll into strips.
    4. Press and roll each dough until flat and thin.
    5. Brush evenly with olive-oil, sprinkle with salt and chopped green onion.
    6. Roll from top to bottom into a strip about 1 cm thick.
    7. Tie the dough into a knot and then flatten into a thin circle.
    8. Heat a saucepan over medium heat, pour in olive-oil
    9. Fry the pancakes on both sides until golden brown.
    10. Serve and enjoy!
