Chocolate Cake

Makes 3 layers for 8" cake pans.

Based on Best-Ever Chocolate Cake with Fudge Frosting By Gemma Stafford (biggerbolderbaking)

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups (250g/9oz) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (400g/14oz) sugar (1.5 cups)
  • 1/2 cup (2oz/75g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup (240ml/8floz) buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup (120g/4oz) vegetable oil, or any flavorless oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml/8floz) freshly brewed hot coffee

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Butter and line 2 8-inch x 2-inch round cake pans.
  3. Sift the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl. Whisk the ingredients together by hand.
  4. In another bowl, combine the buttermilk, oil, eggs, vanilla, and coffee.
  5. Drizzle 1 more tablespoon of olive-oil onto top-side of steak.
  6. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and gently mix the batter just to combine. Do not over mix or beat as this cake can toughen up.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30 to 35 minutes, until a cake tester comes out clean. (Note: this cake cooks fast so check after 25 minutes.)
  8. Cool in the pans for 30 minutes, then turn them out onto a cooling rack and cool completely.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
