French Artisan Bread

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 400g water
  • 10g salt
  • 5g yeast

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix water, flour, and yeast.
  2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 min.
  3. Gently stretch and fold bread in half once on each side.
  4. Let dough rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 (fold and rest) 2 more times.
  6. Sprinkle flour and place dough on baking mat.
  7. Gently stretch and fold top and bottom sides of the dough inwards (form 3 layers).
  8. Stretch left + right ends of the dough outward (make dough longer).
  9. Pinch fold to seal them.
  10. Cut dough into 3 equal pieces.
  11. Put dough into three separate baking pans and rest for 40 more minutes.
  12. Place empty baking pan on lower shelf of oven.
  13. Preheat oven to 450Â°F.
  14. Once preheated, place the dough on the top shelf of the oven and pour 1 cup of hot water into the empty baking pan on the bottom.
  15. Spray water into the oven to generate steam.
  16. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  17. Serve and enjoy!
