Salmon

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet (about 1-inch thick), skin on
  • 3 tablespoons olive-oil
  • sea salt
  • freshly-ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat cast-iron skillet over medium-heat.
  2. Heavily season fillet with salt and black pepper right before cooking.
  3. Drizzle 2 tablespoons of olive-oil on hot skillet.
  4. Lay fillet onto skillet.
  5. Drizzle 1 more tablespoon of olive-oil onto top-side of fillet.
  6. Flip fillet after 2 minutes or until it has a nice sear.
  7. Cover the skillet with a lid and sear for 1 minutes.
  8. Remove the lid, take the skillet off the heat, and let the salmon rest for at least ten minutes.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
